Welcome to my second blog,here,i will share with you my experiences after learning more about Personal Development at Refactory Academy. In the first week,we were introduced to the seven habits which were in the circle below;

I personally gained alot and discovered my hidden weaknesses whereas the advantages to me were as below:

Increased Self-Awareness: Personal development often starts with self-reflection, leading to a deeper understanding of one's values, strengths, weaknesses, and motivations.

Confidence: As individuals identify and build upon their strengths, they often experience a boost in self-confidence and a greater sense of empowerment.

Communication Skills: Personal development often involves learning effective communication techniques, which can improve relationships both personally and professionally.

Better Goal Setting and Time Management: People may develop clearer goals and learn strategies to manage their time more effectively, leading to increased productivity and a greater sense of accomplishment.

Empathy and Understanding: Personal development can foster empathy and a deeper understanding of others' perspectives, leading to stronger relationships and better collaboration.

Stress Reduction: Learning strategies for stress management and self-care can lead to reduced stress levels and improved overall well-being.

Those were the few experiences i encountered as i attended Personal Development lectures and i believe they have lead to positive growth in me.
